FAQ Section

Question 1: Can I access the tests without internet access?

No, our tests require internet access to load.

Question 2: Can my friend use my account?

No, the account is personal and can only be used by one person.

Question 3: Account is not working correctly, what can I do?

Please make sure that you have the latest version of your internet browser. For optimal performance we suggest using Google Chrome.

Question 4: Can I take the tests again and again?

You can take all the tests once and can view their analysis again and again .

Question 5: Is there any limit to the number of tests that I can take in an hour or a day?

No, There is no such limits.

Question 6: Will I get an expert for any subject related query?

No, We do not provide expert guidance. But with all our questions you will get answers.

Question 7: If I face any problem with regard to the website, what should I do?

If you face any problem with regard to the website, you can contact us by sending your message at our Contact Us page.

Question 8: How can I give feedback related to the website?

Your feedback is very valuable to us. Please write to us at our Contact Us page. We look forward to hearing from you.

Question 9: There's an essential feature I want to recommend. What should I do?

We are always open to innovation! It's good that you have ideas for us. Write to us.
